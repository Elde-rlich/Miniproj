HOW TO MEDITATE

Step 1: Find a Comfortable Space

Choose a quiet place where you won't be disturbed. Sit in a comfortable position on a chair or on the floor with a cushion.

Step 2: Focus on Your Breath

Close your eyes and begin to breathe deeply. Focus on the sensation of your breath going in and out. Let your thoughts drift by without getting attached.

Step 3: Observe Your Thoughts

As thoughts arise, acknowledge them, then gently bring your attention back to your breathing. Meditation is about observing without judgment.

Step 4: Gradually Increase Time

Start with a few minutes and gradually increase the time as you get more comfortable with meditation. Aim for consistency over duration.

Step 5: End with Gratitude

When you're ready to end, take a moment to express gratitude for the time you spent in meditation. Slowly open your eyes and bring yourself back to the present.
